## Scoop Strong

Scoop Strong is a custom recipe book I wrote and designed to help those on a fitness journey enjoy a sweet treat while also hitting their protein goals. The book is dedicated to helping users learn how to use the Ninja Creami while making high-protein ice cream recipes that support fitness goals. The goal of the project was to create a fun and practical guide that makes it easy to enjoy a treat while still focusing on balanced nutrition.

The book introduces the device, explains how it works, and walks through simple steps for creating different recipes.
