## Everyday Energy

Everyday Energy is an app that tracks user’s health and offers an approachable app experience. I find that many health apps can be difficult to navigate and non-encompassing, so I created Everyday Energy to put all the important information in one spot. The goal of the project was to create a space for users to access their daily health statistics to aid in their fitness journey and make wellness easy.

The app features organized sections displaying statistics such as mood, sleep, steps, activity, and screen time. It also offers other services such as a mood tracker, sleep tracker, and daily journal. I prioritized the website to have a welcoming prescence and intuitive navigation through its color palette, layut, and typography.
