## Splitli

Splitli is a conceptual website for people seeking guidance on their fitness journey. Fitness progress is often mistaken for just getting active, but Splitli informs users that there is a better method that can
hold accountability, increase progress, and improve daily experience. The goal of the project was to create a non-intimidating experience for new gym-goers and organize helpful content in a digestible and modern format.

The website discusses what a split is, the benefits, a glossary of gym terms, and a form to match users to their best matching split. I prioritized accessibility and user experience in the production of the site, so users from all walks of life can engage in its content. The design features a clean layout, bold color palette, and structured typography to optimize user experience.

## Hyrax Haven

Hyrax Haven is a website for a wildlife conservation organization dedicated to raising awareness about hyraxes. These mammals are small, often overlooked, and are native to Africa and the Middle East. The goal of the project was to transform a basic informational site into a modern digital platform that encourages users to learn about the species and the conservation challenges they face. 

Through research into non-profit websites and content strategy, I developed a site structure that prioritizes education, accessibility, and user engagement. The design organizes information into focused sections, features a clean layout, nature-inspired color palette, and structured typography that presents information in a way that is approachable and visually cohesive.

## Bartender's Glossary

Bartender’s Glossary is a website that delivers a quick guide to create the bar’s most popular drinks and use its tools. As a bartender, I used my years of knowledge to write and design the content for the page. The goal of the project was to create a space for bartenders to educate themselves on how to use their bar to its fullest potential, make drinks that shape the customer experience, and quickly display recipes for those forgetful moments during a shift. 

The website features organized sections displaying cocktail recipes, shot recipes, and explaining bar tools. I prioritized the website to have a quick interaction time with navigation tools and a single page to decrease clicks and improve content delivery time. The website utilizes a simple color palette typography to emphasize the content and improve user experience.
