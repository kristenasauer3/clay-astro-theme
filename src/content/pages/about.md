## The Face Behind The Screen

I’m a graphic designer focused on creating modern, user-centered digital experiences. I recently earned my BFA in Graphic Design from SUNY New Paltz, where I developed a strong foundation in branding, web design, and UX/UI.

Through my work as a Social Media Assistant for the Art History Department, I designed content that reached over 37K monthly Instagram views, combining visual storytelling with strategic thinking. I’m especially interested in designing clean, intuitive interfaces that make information more accessible and engaging.
